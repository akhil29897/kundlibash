# User Guide

No sign-up, no app. Open the site and you're in. Everything you do is kept privately against an anonymous cookie in your browser.

## The three tabs
- **The Reading** — choose your sign and bash today's lines.
- **Almanac** — every note you've kept (accuracy notes + other notes).
- **Reckoning** — your accuracy stats, and the whole zodiac ranked by candour.

## How to bash a horoscope
1. **Choose your sign** under *The Reading*. (It's remembered for next time.)
2. Read **today's reading** — it's broken into numbered lines (I, II, III…).
3. For each line, live your day and then mark it:
   - **rang true** — the day bore it out.
   - **rang hollow** — total hot air.
4. Optionally add an **accuracy note** under a line ("what actually happened") and an **other note** for the day as a whole.
5. Hit **Set down my verdict**. Each line then reveals the **crowd's verdict** — what share of everyone found it true vs hollow.

You can come back and **amend** your verdict any time that day; it won't double-count.

## Your almanac (notes)
- **Accuracy notes** — each line you marked, with its verdict, the original prediction, and what really happened.
- **Other notes** — your free-form day notes.

These build up into a quietly addictive diary of the stars vs. your actual life.

## Your reckoning (stats)
- **Personal:** how often the stars read you true, lines bashed, day streak, days kept, note counts.
- **Community:** every sign ranked by candour, the **truest to life** and **most hollow** signs, and the global hit-rate across everyone.

## Privacy
- No name, no email, no account. You're an anonymous id in a cookie.
- Clear your cookies and you start fresh (your old almanac becomes unreachable).
- **Burn the almanac** (in *Almanac*) erases everything you've kept and pulls your verdicts out of the shared scoreboard. It can't be undone.

## Handy links
- **Open straight to your sign:** `…/?sign=virgo`
- **Open a tab directly:** `…/#reckoning` or `…/#almanac`
- **Guided demo (sample data):** `…/?demo=1` — fills the views with example readings, notes and stats so you can see what it looks like in full. (Demo data is only in your view; it isn't saved.)

## Good to know
- The day's reading is the **same for everyone with your sign** — that's what makes the community scoreboard a fair contest.
- A new reading appears each day (Indian Standard Time).
- It's for diversion, not counsel. The whole point is to doubt it properly.
